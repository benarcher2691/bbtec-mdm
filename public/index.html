ðŸ“– How to Enroll an Android Device

  1. Factory Reset your Android device (or use a new device)
  2. Start the setup wizard - at the welcome screen
  3. Tap the screen 6 times to launch the QR code scanner
  4. Connect to Wi-Fi when prompted
  5. Scan the QR code generated above
  6. Wait for the device to download and apply the policy
  7. Complete the setup - your device is now enrolled!
Note: This requires Android 7.0+ and works best with devices that support QR provisioning. The device will download Google's Android Device Policy app automatically.
